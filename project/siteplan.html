Come And Bake - Site Plan

Site Name

Come And Bake

This name represents a blog that focuses on baking recipes, tips, and stories. It's meant to convey a welcoming and warm atmosphere for baking enthusiasts.

Optional domain availability: comeandbake.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for baking enthusiasts to find recipes, tips, and inspiration we also follow seasons to make things spicy. It will include a variety of baking recipes, a blog for baking stories, and a form for visitors to submit their own recipes or share feedback.

Scenarios

  • What are some easy baking recipes for beginners?
  • How can I get in the mood for baking without feeling overwhelmed and unprepared?

Color Schema

  • #ff6347 - Purple: Used for headings and accents.
  • #fffacd - White: Used for background and highlights.

Typography

  • Header Font: 'Sofia', cursive - Used for main headings.
  • Body Font: 'Montserrat Alternates', sans-serif - Used for body text and paragraphs.
