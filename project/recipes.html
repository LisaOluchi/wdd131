Ingredients:
1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
1/2 cup brewed coffee or 1 shot of espresso
1 tablespoon maple syrup
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:
Heat the milk in a small saucepan over medium heat until steaming, but not boiling.
Brew the coffee or espresso.
In a mug, combine the hot coffee, maple syrup, and ground cinnamon.
Pour the steamed milk into the mug, holding back the foam with a spoon. Stir to combine.
Spoon the foam on top of the latte.
Top with whipped cream and an extra sprinkle of cinnamon, if desired.
Serve immediately.

Ingredients:
1 can of refrigerated crescent roll dough
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/4 cup sugar
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup powdered sugar
2 tablespoons milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:
Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).
Unroll the crescent roll dough and separate it into triangles.
Mix the sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.
Brush the melted butter onto the dough triangles.
Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly over the dough.
Roll up each triangle starting from the wide end to the narrow end.
Place the rolls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
Mix the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract to make a glaze.
Drizzle the glaze over the warm cinnamon rolls.
Serve warm.
